# **3\. PLATFORM DESCRIPTION, NATURE, AND SCOPE OF SERVICES**

## **3.1 Nature of the Platform**

The VAYUXI ERP Platform is a software-as-a-service tool designed exclusively to assist construction companies, contractors, engineers, project managers, and site supervisors in digitising, organising, and managing construction site operations, project data, and administrative workflows. The Platform provides a digital interface for data entry, reporting, tracking, and document management in relation to construction projects.

## **3.2 Modules Included**

Subject to the Customer's Subscription Plan, the Platform may include the following modules:

3.2.1 Site Progress Tracking: A module enabling Authorized Users to record and monitor construction progress against predefined milestones, activities, or schedules entered by the Customer.

3.2.2 Daily Progress Reports (DPR): A module enabling Authorized Users to create, upload, and archive daily site reports including workforce attendance, work completed, material consumed, and site observations.

3.2.3 Material Tracking: A module for recording material procurement, receipt, storage, consumption, and wastage at project sites as entered by Authorized Users.

3.2.4 Billing and Rate Uploads: A module enabling the Customer to upload rate schedules, billing statements, invoices, and financial data for tracking purposes. This module does not constitute accounting software, financial advisory, or a certified accounting system.

3.2.5 Contractor Management: A module for registering, managing, and tracking contractor details, work assignments, and performance data as entered by the Customer.

3.2.6 Floor and MOC Management: A module facilitating documentation and management of floor-level construction activities and method-of-construction records.

3.2.7 Project-Based Workflows: Project-centric organisational tools enabling the creation and management of discrete projects with associated tasks, timelines, and team members.

3.2.8 Media Uploads: A feature enabling Authorized Users to upload photographs, videos, PDF documents, drawings, and other digital files in connection with a Project, subject to storage limits and acceptable use requirements.
